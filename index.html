Nexovia

The system wasn’t built for us.
Nexovia is a system of care designed to support women through every stage of life — with data, insight, and real compassion.

Why Nexovia

Most health systems were built on male data. They fail to capture the lived realities of women’s cycles, transitions, and hormonal shifts. Nexovia exists to fill that structural gap — not with another tracker, but with a system designed to understand and evolve with women’s bodies.

What We’re Building

  • 📊 Biosensor inputs (sleep, HRV, temperature, mood)
  • 🧬 Life-phase-specific insight models
  • 🌙 Emotion-aware, adaptive tracking tools
  • 🔐 A private, supportive space to reflect and connect

We’re starting with perimenopause — and building toward a continuum of care from puberty to longevity.

Current Prototype

We’re in early prototyping mode: logging biosignals, testing interpretation methods, and validating the experience with real-world data. Follow development in our prototype repository.
